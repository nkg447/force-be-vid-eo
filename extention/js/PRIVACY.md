# Privacy Policy for Force be Vid-eo | Control Video Remotely

**Last Updated: March 30, 2025**

## Introduction

Thank you for choosing "Force be Vid-eo | Control Video Remotely" ("the Extension"), a Chrome extension that enables you to control videos playing in your browser from your mobile device by scanning a QR code. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use and protect that information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

The Extension does not require any personal information such as name, email address, or account creation to function.

### Information Collected Automatically

When you use the Extension, we automatically collect the following information:

- **Session Data**: Temporary connection information required to establish and maintain the remote control session between your browser and mobile device.
- **Video Metadata**: Basic information about the video being played, such as playback status, current time, and duration. This information is necessary for the remote control functionality to work properly.
- **QR Code Usage**: We record when a QR code is generated and scanned, but we do not track which device scanned the code.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

- We do not collect personal identifiers (name, email, phone number).
- We do not collect or store the content of the videos you watch.
- We do not track browsing history beyond the tab where the Extension is active.
- We do not collect payment information.
- We do not create user profiles or persistent identifiers.

## How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

1. Establish and maintain the remote control connection between your browser and mobile device.
2. Enable the core functionality of controlling video playback remotely.
3. Improve the Extension's performance and fix bugs.

## Data Storage and Retention

- **Session Data**: All session data is temporary and exists only during the active connection between your browser and mobile device. This data is deleted once you close the browser tab, disable the Extension, or end the remote control session.
- **Local Storage**: Some settings preferences may be stored locally on your device for convenience. This data never leaves your device.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share any information collected by the Extension with third parties. We may only disclose information if required to do so by law or if necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others.

## Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information collected through the Extension against unauthorized or unlawful processing and against accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

- **Control**: You can disable the Extension at any time through Chrome's extension settings.
- **Delete**: All session data is automatically deleted when you end a session or disable the Extension.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will post the updated Privacy Policy on the Chrome Web Store and, if the changes are significant, provide a more prominent notice.

## Special Note About Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed to children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Browser Permissions

The Extension requires the following permissions to function:

- **Tab Access**: To detect and control video playback in the active tab.
- **Network Access**: To establish the remote control connection between your browser and mobile device.
- **QR Code Generation**: To create the QR code that links your devices.

These permissions are necessary for the core functionality of the Extension and are not used for any other purpose.

## Additional Information for Specific Jurisdictions

### For European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. As the Extension does not collect personal data that can identify you, these rights are limited in application.

### For California Users (CCPA/CPRA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), California residents have certain rights regarding their personal information. Since the Extension does not collect personal information that identifies you, sell your personal information, or share your personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising, most CCPA/CPRA provisions do not apply.

### For Users in Other Jurisdictions

The Extension is designed to comply with privacy laws worldwide by minimizing data collection to only what is necessary for functionality.
